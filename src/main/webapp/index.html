A bot for Typetalkâ„¢, that has the following functions:

  • Greetings;
  • Report the current weather conditions for workers at Fukuoka-shi;
  • Translate words and phrases from English to Portuguese.

How to

  1. Create your bot with Webhook URL for your topic (docs)
  2. For the URL, use: http://will1bot.herokuapp.com/webhook/
  3. After the bot is added to a topic, send a message mentioning him, i. e., beginning with @botname+

The supported commands are in section Commands

Commands

  1. Greetings: "@botname+ hello", e. g., "@will1bot hello"
  2. Weather: "@botname+ weather", e. g., "@will1bot weather"
  3. Translate EN-PT: "@botname+ translate phrase", e. g., "@will1bot translate I love sushi"
